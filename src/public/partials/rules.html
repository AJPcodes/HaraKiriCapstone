How to play

Each player has a 2 x 3 grid of 6 cards.

The object of the game is to have the lowest score at the end of 9 rounds.

Substitute your cards for lower value cards or make matches to win.

Most cards are worth their face value. e.g. 8 is 8 points, 4 is 4 points, and an Ace is 1 point.

All face-cards are worth 10 points

2s are special, and are scored as negative 2 points.

Game Play

At the start of a round, each players is dealt a new grid of 2 cards shown face-up and 4 face-down.

Players alternate drawing the top card of either the deck or discard pile and either substituting them (face-up) for cards in their grids or discarding them.

Matched cards of the same value in a 2 card collumn (e.g. a pair of 8s or a pair of 3s) are scored as 0 points.

The round ends when one player has all 6 cards shown face up.
